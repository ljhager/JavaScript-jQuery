Open Links in a New Tab


Internal Links

Hover over an interior link and the value of its respective HREF will display as a tool-tip. The URL is also displayed at the bottom right of the page. Click on a link and it will open on a new tab. Since there are no numbered pages in this file a "Cannot GET" message will appear on the new tab. However, clicking on the Home link will open this site's Home page on another tab.

External Links

Hover over an external link and its web address will display at the bottom right of the page. Click on a link and the web page will open on a new tab.
